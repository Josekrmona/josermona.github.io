Recipe#1

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 3und
Butter 2 cups
Eggs 3 und
Feta Cheese 300 g
Fresh dill or parsley to garnish 1 und

Step 1: Spread a thin layer of softened butter on both sides of the bread. Fry the bread over medium heat for about 2 minutes on each side, until a golden crust forms.

Step 2:Remove the bread from the frying pan and add 1/2 tbsp. of butter. Add the eggs and feta cheese and cook, stirring often, for about 3-4 minutes, until the eggs are cooked through.

Step:3Divide the eggs between the bread slices and sprinkle them with freshly chopped dill or parsley.

Step:44. Make yourself a cup of coffee and enjoy The Best Scrambled Egg Toast.
